Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, and password (stored as a secure hash)
  • Shipping Data: Shipment details, carrier selections, tracking information, and rate quotes you request
  • Payment Information: Payment method details you provide (bank accounts, credit cards, etc.)
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, API calls made, and interaction patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the PacketBase platform
  • Process payments and generate invoices
  • Generate analytics and insights about your shipping operations
  • Send notifications about shipment status, billing, and account activity
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Respond to support requests and communicate with you

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely with encryption at rest and in transit. Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2-HMAC with 100,000 iterations. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Shipping Carriers: To facilitate shipment creation and tracking
  • Payment Processors: To process your payments securely
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, subpoena, or legal process

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your data at any time
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use session cookies to maintain your login state. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels. Analytics data is collected and processed internally.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, we retain anonymized analytics data but remove all personally identifiable information within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

PacketBase is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
